# How to impress Ring Nepal’s owner (and close the website)

**Goal:** They say “yes, we want this as our official site” (or paid Phase 1).  
**Your live demo:** Vercel URL from project `ring-nepal`  
**Tone:** Helpful partner, not pushy salesperson. Show, don’t lecture.

---

## 1. What the owner actually cares about

| They care about | How you prove it on the site |
|-----------------|------------------------------|
| More sales without more rent | Browse 24/7 → Order on WhatsApp in 1 tap |
| Looks as pretty as Instagram | Hero video + product videos + soft pink brand |
| Staff won’t be confused | Pre-filled WhatsApp message (product + price) |
| They can update products themselves | **Admin panel** — show live add/edit |
| Multi-store is clear | All 8 stores listed + call / WhatsApp |
| Low risk | Start with WhatsApp (they already sell that way) |
| Not locked into you forever | Simple site, export catalog JSON, own domain later |

**One sentence pitch:**

> “I built a digital showroom for Ring Nepal — customers shop like Instagram, order like WhatsApp, and you control products from a simple admin. No full e‑commerce headache on day one.”

---

## 2. Before the meeting (prepare once)

- [ ] Open site on **your phone** (4G), not only laptop  
- [ ] Tap every **Order** → WhatsApp opens with product text  
- [ ] Hero + product videos play muted  
- [ ] Open **`/admin`** → login → change a product name → Save → refresh shop  
- [ ] Know your **live URL** (copy it short)  
- [ ] Screenshots ready (home, product, WhatsApp, admin) if internet fails  
- [ ] Optional: print 1 page “What you get” (section 6 below)

**Do not** lead with domain or SEO. Lead with **sales + look + control**.

---

## 3. Demo script (8–10 minutes)

### Minute 0–1 — Hook (phone first)
Hand them your phone or theirs:

> “Search is full of Instagram. Customers still can’t browse your full catalog cleanly. This is what an official Ring Nepal site can look like.”

Open the homepage. Let the **hero video** play. Pause. Let them react.

### Minute 1–3 — “Like Instagram, but shoppable”
1. Scroll **showreel** (horizontal videos)  
2. Open a **product** → Quick view  
3. Tap **Order on WhatsApp**  

> “Customer already decided. Staff get name + price in one message. No ‘which ring was it?’ ”

### Minute 3–5 — Multi-store + trust
Scroll to **Stores**.

> “All branches in one place. Call or WhatsApp with store name ready for pickup.”

Point to **How to order** (3 steps) and delivery/COD note.

### Minute 5–7 — The killer: Admin
Open `/admin` on your laptop (bigger screen).

1. Login  
2. Edit one product or mark “new”  
3. **Save**  
4. Open shop in another tab — show change  

> “Your team can update prices and photos without calling a developer every time.”

### Minute 7–9 — Why not full cart yet
Use simple language from `WHY-WHATSAPP-MODEL.md`:

> “Nepal jewelry buyers trust chat. Full cart + payment is Phase 2 when volume is high. Phase 1 makes you money with what you already do well.”

### Minute 9–10 — Close
> “If you like this direction, I can put your real photos, prices, and WhatsApp number live in about a week. Shall we lock Phase 1?”

**Soft closes (pick one):**
- “Can we go live with Baneshwor WhatsApp first?”  
- “Send me 10 product photos + prices this week?”  
- “Who on your team will manage admin?”

## 5. Pricing ideas (adjust to your market)

Suggest **packages**, not hourly (owners hate vague hours).

| Package | Includes | Suggest framing |
|---------|----------|-----------------|
| **Phase 1 — Launch** | This site live, their photos/prices, WhatsApp, stores, domain connect, 1 training on admin | One fixed fee + optional small monthly care |
| **Care (optional)** | Small monthly: updates, festival banners, support | “So you’re never stuck” |
| **Phase 2 later** | Cart, eSewa/Khalti, SMS | Quote separately when ready |

**Nepal-friendly framing:**
- One-time setup = “building the shop window”  
- Monthly = “keeping the lights on” (optional, small)  
- Phase 2 = only when orders overflow WhatsApp  

**Negotiation tips:**
- Free concept already built = goodwill; paid work = go-live + training + their data  
- Offer **50% start / 50% on go-live**  
- If they want free forever: “Concept is free to review; go-live + training is paid.”

## 6. One-page “What you get” (say this out loud)

**Ring Nepal website — Phase 1**

1. Premium homepage with brand video  
2. Product catalog with video/photo  
3. WhatsApp order buttons (message pre-filled)  
4. All store locations  
5. How-to-order + delivery notes  
6. Mobile-first (most customers on phone)  
7. Admin panel for products/stock  
8. Ready for your real domain later  

**Not in Phase 1 (honest):**  
Full cart, online payment gateway, customer accounts — Phase 2.

## 7. Objections & replies

| They say | You say |
|----------|---------|
| “We have Instagram” | “IG is great for reach. Site is the permanent catalog + one-tap order. Link it in bio.” |
| “Customers only come to store” | “Many only visit to *look*. Let them look online; store for try/pickup.” |
| “We need payment online first” | “We can add eSewa/Khalti in Phase 2. Most jewelry chats still close on WhatsApp.” |
| “Looks expensive / complicated” | “You already use WhatsApp. This only organizes it. I’ll train your staff 30 min.” |
| “Domain / Google search?” | “We go live first. Domain + Google later. Sales don’t wait for SEO.” |
| “Can we change colors/photos?” | “Yes — admin for products; design tweaks included in Phase 1 go-live.” |
| “What if we stop with you?” | “Site + catalog export stay with you. No hostage hosting.” |

## 8. Follow-up sequence (if no reply)

| When | Channel | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| Day 0 | Email + IG DM | Short message + link |
| Day 3 | IG story reply / WhatsApp if public | “Did you get a chance to open on mobile?” |
| Day 7 | Visit **one store** (politely) | Show on phone, leave URL on note |
| Day 14 | Last soft message | “Happy to adjust design free if you share 2 feedback points” |

Never spam daily.

---

## 9. Live demo checklist (day of meeting)

1. Full battery, hotspot ready  
2. Site loaded once (cache) before you enter store  
3. WhatsApp installed and logged in  
4. Admin password changed from default if showing on their device  
5. Browser bookmarks: Home · Admin  
6. Smile — you’re offering value, not begging  

---

- [ ] They send 10+ product photos + prices  
- [ ] They name decision-maker + next call time  
- [ ] Written “yes to Phase 1” on WhatsApp
